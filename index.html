About Me

My name is Jeff McCormick. I am a student at thinkful.com. I joined back in February 2016 and finished the front end web developer course in June 2016. My experience with webdesign is 2 classes online at National American University in 2013. I learned how to build a website in HTML, CSS, and Javascript. I had to build my own portfolio which is need of updating. I also had to make my own Wordpress Blog. I have also been helping a non-profit since 2014 called Crossroads for Women. I helped them add new pages on their redesigned website, created a slider, and learned about hosting through Bluehost. In my spare time. I spend most of the spring and summer watching my twin boys play baseball. My son Zach plays club baseball for the Hooligan's organization in Rio Rancho, NM. He also plays Little League at Paradise Hills in Albuqueruque, NM. My son Nick plays baseball at Paradise Hills Little League. I enjoy doing any type of outdoor activities with my family, serving in the media area my church Sagebrush Community Church, monitoring the lyrics for worship, and changing the slides using Propresenter for the Pastors multimedia presentation. I like to stay up to date with modern technology. I also enjoy social media such as Facebook, Twitter, Linkedin, and Instagram.
